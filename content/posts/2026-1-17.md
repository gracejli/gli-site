a learning: being in flow state != passion (convo about the book "perfection" with sarah), and is rather just about focus

i think instead of using "flow state" as a filter for what you are passionate about, flow state should rather be used as a filter to understand 

flow state is just _any_ period you are focused: creating, writing, playing music, playing sport

like how coding for yourself, for a website is flow state just as much how coding for your job is flow state 

and the reason why we herald flow state now, find it holy, is just because we are so distracted most of the time, that flow state feels like the outlier
