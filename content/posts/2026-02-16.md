Linocut event today, and a LNY hotpot with some work friends. Both of these events today were with groups of people, friends, who the majority I do not know and was meeing for the first time.

It’s nice to move between groups, make things, enjoy food. People are all quite generous and amazing. On the drive back from the west side I was thinking about how I heard about the “chinese banquet” wedding, twice, one from a friend who was about to attend her childhood friend’s wedding in May, in China, and the other from someone who just proposed last year, who was from Malaysia, and had three events planned, all timed around the superstitious Chinese New Year calendar. One event in southern California, one in northern California, and finally, the Chinese banquet back in Malaysia. I've always wondered what a Chinese wedding could look like, I've never been to one myself. It's nice to think that Chinese people like to celebrate with food and with many people in more ways than one.
