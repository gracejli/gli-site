camping in the sequoia national forest, at potwisha campground. open tent, no rainfly the second night. i think about how lucky this is, to have two evenings where nothing of the outdoors triggers any homeostatis response. food is abundant, no bugs, quiet outside, nothing needed but a jacket. our eyes close under the stars. the trees are bigger than you could ever imagine. 

met our camping neighbors, a group of friends who spoke tamil and made fresh chai in the morning. they shared a cup with 9999 and I. i leave this weekend thinking to myself it's wifi that is public enemy number one. and that it's also important to feel small.
