v1 of blog deployed
you can learn so much just by creating again 
the biggest impetus to move away from my old cargo site was actually the need for the blog/notes component

i used to think flow state, and time passing meant that this thing you were doing was your passion 

and you could discern things you liked vs things you didn't by flow-state-ness

but i think flow state actually more or less equals time spent really creating something 

which might not mean this thing is your passion (which is okay, you can evaluate the "product" after you are done)

but perhaps just means that creating something, focusing, is just a nice and positive thing to do in your life, whatever the product is.
